Country ranking (home)

This ranking takes into account all battles over the past year that meet the following requirements: 1 vs 1 or 2 vs 2, pro or open level, 15+ or no age restriction, and only battles won by a dancer who is from the country where it's organised. The points per country is the sum of the points acquired by the dancers representing each country. The ranking is updated daily.
