Country ranking

This ranking takes into account all battles over the past year that meet the following requirements: 1 vs 1 or 2 vs 2, pro or open level, 15+ or no age restriction, and no female-only battles. The points per country is the sum of the points acquired by the dancers representing each country. The ranking is updated daily.
